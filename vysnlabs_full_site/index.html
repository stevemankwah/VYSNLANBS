Secure Digital Vehicle Identification Infrastructure

VYSNLABS designs and delivers NFC and RFID-enabled vehicle identification systems that support secure registration, enforcement, and mobility operations at national and regional scale.

Our solutions combine certified hardware, secure software architecture, and systems integration expertise to meet the operational, regulatory, and security requirements of public authorities.

Solutions

VYSNLABS provides end-to-end vehicle identification solutions designed to support government agencies, regulatory authorities, and authorised operators throughout the vehicle lifecycle.

NFC / RFID-Enabled Number Plates

Secure aluminium vehicle number plates embedded with RFID or NFC identifiers to enable reliable, tamper-resistant vehicle identification.

  • Embedded secure chips with unique, non-replicable identifiers
  • Tamper-evident construction and anti-counterfeiting features
  • Designed for long-term durability under local conditions
  • Compatibility with national registration databases

Electronic Vehicle Identification Systems

Digital vehicle identity systems enabling automated verification across controlled and regulated environments.

  • Roadside and checkpoint enforcement
  • Border and customs verification
  • Tolling, congestion charging, and zonal access
  • Fleet and regulated vehicle monitoring

Software, Security & Systems Integration

Backend platforms and integration services connecting vehicle identifiers with registration systems, enforcement tools, and analytics dashboards.

  • Secure APIs for interoperability
  • Role-based access control and audit logging
  • Encryption at rest and in transit
  • Scalable architecture for nationwide deployments
